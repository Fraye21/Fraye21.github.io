My Experience

Saving money is a choice. Either you do it because you want to, or you do it because you have to. My own experience with saving has lead me to eventually getting the stuff that I intentionally saved for.

How to Start Saving Money:

  • Minimize spending to account for only what YOU yourself think is essential.
  • A good metric for saving is about half of half what you use in a day.
  • Some savings is better than no savings at all.
